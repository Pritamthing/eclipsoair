EclipsoAir is Pet Friendly

To travel you must make sure that your documentation is correct. Interisland is a little different to a non-UK entity incoming flight. YOU must make sure for all flights that you have:

  1. An up-to-date GB or EU passport and that all vaccinations are up to date and the animal is chipped.

  2. Dogs flying into the Channel Islands or the UK must have had a tapeworm tablet within 4 days of travel.

  3. When flying to the Channel Islands for the first time, you must have a Veterinary Health Certificate issued within the last 90 days.

  4. If flying in from another country or continent such as the EU, you must have the above requirements and use a licensed carrier. EclipsoAir is a licensed carrier.

All we ask is that if you want to travel with an animal please book via our On Demand Charter Service.

On Demand How It Works:

An individual can charter a flight specifying single or return, departure airport and return, using our application and opt whether or not the flight can be shared.

If additional passengers book then the tickets will automatically discount first passenger, second passenger, third, fourth, fifth, sixth.

Safety is Our Core Value

Safety is one of Gama’s core values and fundamental to the way we conduct our activities. The safety of clients and employees is our primary focus, and we strive to achieve this by promoting a positive and proactive safety culture.

Gama Aviation is committed to:

  • Maintaining and improving our aviation safety performance and minimizing, as far as reasonably practicable, the risk of incidents and accidents.
  • Our Management Team promoting safety as a primary responsibility throughout their daily activities and ensuring that all employees have the training and resources to perform their work safely.
  • Ensuring that all employees understand their safety and reporting responsibilities and accountabilities through accepted policies and procedures.
  • Promoting and practicing a “Just Culture” which supports an uninhibited flow of any information that may affect safety.
  • Proactive hazard identification and risk management.
  • Maintaining compliance with industry safety standards, best practices, and all regulatory requirements.
  • Ensuring that the Safety Management System is proactively monitored and regularly reviewed for effectiveness and continuous improvement.

Through the personal commitment of all our employees, we are dedicated to providing the highest level of safety across the organization.

Frequently Asked   Questions

Our Frequently Asked Questions section aims to provide you with detailed information about our services, membership options, and booking process to help you better understand how EclipsoAir works and to ensure a seamless travel experience.

What is EclipsoAir?

EclipsoAir is a luxury private airline offering short-haul and inter-island flights. We provide an exclusive service for individuals, families, and corporate clients with flexible options for regular flights, private charters, and on-demand packages.

How do I book a flight?

Register with us and effortlessly book your flight with just a click on Book a Flight.

How do I become a member of EclipsoAir?

You can choose from different membership options:
1. Corporate Membership: Register your business and provide details of passengers entitled to book flights.
2. Family Membership: Choose between a subscription or pay-as-you-go on-demand service for your family.
3. Individual Membership: Register yourself and select a subscription or pay-as-you-go option.

What is the cost of membership?

- Corporate Membership: £36,000, which includes 45 tickets.
- Family Membership: Choose between a subscription or on-demand pay-as-you-go service.
- Individual Membership: Choose a subscription or pay-as-you-go option for your flights.

How can I book a flight with EclipsoAir?

Flights can be booked through our online platform. Depending on your membership type, you can book regular flights, private charters, or on-demand services. Corporate members can select passengers from a pre-approved list.

Can I book a shared flight?

Yes, use our on demand or membership solution.

What happens if I use all my allocated tickets?

Once all your tickets are used, you can apply and pay for another batch of tickets. Corporate members have a client account, from which funds are drawn when booking additional flights.

How does the on-demand pricing model work?

EclipsoAir offers a unique pricing model where the base cost decreases as more passengers book a flight. For example, the first passenger pays the full ticket price, while additional passengers receive a discounted rate, making it more affordable for everyone.

What aircraft does EclipsoAir use?

EclipsoAir primarily uses the Cessna Caravan 208 , chosen for its operational efficiency and environmentally friendly features. It is a single-engine aircraft, consuming less fuel compared to twin-engine models.

Is EclipsoAir environmentally friendly?

Yes, we are committed to minimizing our environmental impact. The Cessna Caravan 208 we use has lower CO2 emissions compared to other aircraft operating in the region, and its single engine reduces fuel consumption.

Is personal data stored on the website?

No, no personal data is stored on the website. We ensure that your privacy and data security are of the utmost importance and follow strict guidelines.

How do I contact EclipsoAir for support?

For inquiries or support, you can reach us via the contact page on our website. We also provide live chat support to assist with any questions or issues you may have.
